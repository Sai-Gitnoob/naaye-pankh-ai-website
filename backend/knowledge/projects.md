15\. Core Projects & Community Welfare Programs
-----------------------------------------------

NayePankh operates several targeted initiatives driven by its youth network to uplift marginalized groups:

*   **Food Security & Stray Animal Feeding:**
    
    *   **Eradicating Hunger:** Regular food distribution drives in slums and roadside communities.
        
    *   **Nutritional Support:** Distribution of fresh food packets, healthy snacks, and essential ration packs directly to street children.
        
    *   **Animal Welfare:** Hunger mitigation efforts are extended to feeding stray animals across operating cities.
        
*   **Education & Youth Literacy:**
    
    *   **Primary Learning:** Educational outreach for children unable to afford formal schooling due to financial barriers.
        
    *   **Resource Provision:** Campaigns and donation drives to provide textbooks, stationery items, and learning infrastructure to marginalized students.
        
*   **Menstrual Hygiene & Women Empowerment:**
    
    *   **Awareness Campaigns:** Educating young women and youths in public and rural areas to break social taboos around menstruation and improve personal health.
        
    *   **Sanitary Kit Distribution:** Providing free sanitary napkins to underprivileged girls to ensure health safety and hygiene.
        
*   **Clothes for Hope (Dignity Drives):**
    
    *   **Clothing Distribution:** Regular donation drives to supply clean, well-kept clothes and warm winter garments to poor families and roadside dwellers, promoting comfort and self-respect.
        
*   **Healthcare Initiatives:**
    
    *   **Basic Medical Aid:** Ensuring marginalized families have basic medical resources, access to healthcare, and clean sanitation awareness.
        
    *   **Emergency Response:** Historically managed localized health drives and vaccination support during major community crises like the COVID-19 pandemic.
