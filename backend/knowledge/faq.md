21\. Detailed Donation & Subscription Policy
--------------------------------------------

*   **Voluntary Contribution:** Donations are strictly voluntary, intended solely for charitable purposes, and are not payments for goods or services.
    
*   **Payment Gateway Partner:** NayePankh utilizes **Razorpay** for processing donations. Razorpay is PCI-DSS compliant and ensures end-to-end encryption. The Foundation does not store bank or card credentials on its servers.
    
*   **Recurring Subscriptions:** Enrolling in recurring donations authorizes NayePankh to collect funds automatically at fixed intervals (monthly/quarterly/yearly).
    
*   **Subscription Cancellation Process:**
    
    *   Donors can cancel future recurring charges at any time through their Razorpay account or by emailing contact@nayepankh.com with their transaction ID and registered email.
        
    *   Cancellations take **3–5 working days** to reflect in the Razorpay system.
        
    *   _Example 1:_ If a monthly ₹500 subscription is cancelled on the 10th of the month, the payment already processed remains non-refundable, but future deductions will stop.
        
    *   _Example 2:_ Cancelling after an annual ₹5,000 donation is processed stops future automatic renewals, but the processed amount is non-refundable.
        

22\. Strict "No Refund" and Chargeback Policy
---------------------------------------------

*   **Immediate Allocation:** NayePankh enforces a strict **no-refund and no-exchange policy**. All one-time and recurring contributions are final.
    
*   **Operational Reason:** Funds are deployed immediately upon receipt to sustain ongoing community projects (daily meals, tuition fees, sanitation supplies). Reversing donations would directly jeopardize active beneficiaries.
    
*   **Chargebacks & Disputes:** If a donor raises a chargeback dispute with their bank, NayePankh will submit proof of donation authorization and fund utilization. Banks generally deny these chargebacks as contributions are purely voluntary.
    

23\. Privacy, Data Protection & Rights
--------------------------------------

*   **Collected Information:** NayePankh collects names, email addresses, phone numbers, addresses, and PAN numbers (exclusively for generating 80G tax receipts). Non-personal data (IP address, browser type, cookies) is collected for performance analytics.
    
*   **Data Sharing Boundaries:** Data is never sold, rented, or traded. It is shared strictly with Razorpay (for processing), trusted IT vendors, or government tax authorities when legally required.
    
*   **Donor Data Rights:** Donors hold the right to request access to their personal data, correct inaccuracies, withdraw marketing consent, or request data deletion (subject to tax/compliance retention laws).
