7\. Leadership
--------------

*   **Prashant Shukla:** Founder & President, NayePankh Foundation.
    

8\. Founder's Message & Core Philosophy
---------------------------------------

*   **Campaign Slogan:** "Together, Let's Make a Difference!"
    
*   **The Vision:** To break the cycle of poverty and hopelessness for marginalized communities. The foundation operates on the strong belief that every child has the right to dream and achieve their aspirations, regardless of their socio-economic background.
    
*   **The Approach:** Empowering individuals to lead better lives and realize their true potential by providing a ray of hope and essential support.
    

9\. Donations & Impact ("Why Donate?")
--------------------------------------

NayePankh relies on the generosity of supporters to reach more communities. The foundation emphasizes that even the smallest contributions create a massive ripple effect of kindness and can serve as a lifeline for those struggling to survive.

**What Donations Accomplish:**

*   **Educational Support:** Provides children with school uniforms, shoes, and the means to overcome financial or infrastructural constraints keeping them out of school.
    
*   **Healthcare & Sanitation:** Grants families critical access to clean water, proper sanitation facilities, and essential healthcare.
    
*   **Basic Necessities:** Ensures vulnerable individuals receive nutritious meals, food, and shelter support.
